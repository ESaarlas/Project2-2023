My Top 5 Songs

#1. Rooster (2022 Remaster) - Alice in Chains

Alice in Chains' "Rooster," in its 2022 remastered glory, showcases the enduring influence of this iconic grunge band.

#2. Self Discovery - Habe

Habe's "Self Discovery" might not be a well-known track, but this emerging artist is making waves in the electronic music scene.

#3. Hold My Liquor - Kanye West

Kanye West, one of the most influential and controversial figures in the music industry, delivered "Hold My Liquor" as part of his groundbreaking "Yeezus" album.

#4. Lemon Tree - Mt. Joy

Mt. Joy, an indie folk-rock band, has been steadily making a name for themselves with their heartfelt and relatable music.

#5. Go! - M83

M83, the brainchild of French musician Anthony Gonzalez, is known for their dreamy and cinematic soundscapes.
